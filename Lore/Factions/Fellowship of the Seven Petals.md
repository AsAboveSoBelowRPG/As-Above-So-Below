The Fellowship of the Seven Petals is a group of adventurers that formed in the village of [[Conexão]] in the [[Luavila (Region)|capital region]] of [[Iberys]] in the year 3'3237 by the legendary hero [[Bixo das Flores]]. Together with his oldest friend [[Marçal Passante]], he gathered a group of adventurers bound together by prophetic dreams to cleanse the valley of a creeping darkness.

Since the founding the fellowship, the group has expanded as the members grew, took partners, expanded their circles and had children. What began as a close group of seven individuals continues to grow, even now. Though some members have drifted away from the group's center of activity, the [[House of Blossoms]], the core group has remained together - more of a family than anything else.

# Foundation

When a wave of prophetic dreams swept through the village in 3'3237, Bixo and Marçal decided to investigate and see if there were any threads tying them together. These five dreamers became the core of the group, along with Marçal and Frida. After they cast the darkness from the valley, the group found themselves in a pocket dimension that seemed to expand to suit their needs. This land outside of time became their base of operations; its main gate exiting onto the streets of [[Conexão]] through the door that used to belong to Bixo's home.

# Members

Because the fellowship is a growing and expanding organization that welcomes those drawn into its orbit, its roster is always growing and diminishing as members come and go. No one is beholden to remain in the house beyond when they feel compelled to stay, and members are always encouraged to pursuit their dreams in or outside of the fellowship.

# Adventures

Most of the adventures of the fellowship are officially cataloged by [[Lina das Flores]], who has been keeping an avid journal of the group's history since she was eight years old. This series of histories started appearing in the [[Academy of Narrative Cosmology]]'s library before she re-wrote them specifically for publication at the insistence of her partner [[Kavita Vahamonde das Flores]] who was horrified to find intimate details of their personal lives from Lina's private journal being read by her students. 

The series, which still carries the name of her first journal, is called [[The Little Hero Who Could See Dreams (Series)]]. Her private journals are well beyond 100 volumes, but the officially published series has considerably fewer books.
