A mysterious mystical being that inhabits the dangerous woodland between the villages of [[Aldeia de Raiz]] and [[Aldeia de Espiga]], Veada is a woodland satyr that has long called a small cavern her home. This simple existence has recently been challenged by a brutal Weredog that has moved into the woodlands and rallied the wolves into her vicious pack. These wolves have taken her home as their den, leaving her cast into the wilderness.

Against her better judgement, she snuck into the nearby village to seek assistance with her problem. She found a friendly orc by the name of [[Morgamorgar]] who offered to help, but ultimately left to return to the village. Whether or not he will return to make good on his word remains to be seen.
