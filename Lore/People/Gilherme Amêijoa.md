One of the most influential and beloved people in town, Gil considers himself to be the power behind the scenes in the village of Aldeia de Raiz - finding that most of the citizens approach him with their problems before they go to [[Rodrigo De Maya]]. He has a large personality and a larger laugh - always looking to bring a little light into the lives of the people in his community.

Gil serves as the village barber-surgeon, handling everything from a shave and a haircut to stitches, or even serious surgery. Though he seems quite aloof and unprofessional, when it comes to his profession he takes it very seriously and is quite proficient with both shears and a knife.

Despite his station as a well respected man and a skilled doctor, Gil has maintained his lifestyle as a bachelor; never getting involved romantically with the women in town.
