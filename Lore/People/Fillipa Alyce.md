Fillipa is a young archeologist who works for [[Quatrograde]] university. Following in the footsteps of her disgraced mentor, [[Dušan Wright]], she seeks to prove the existence of an extinct species of ancient dwarf known as the [[Vanda]] who were believed to inhabit the [[Kasmanium Range]].

During the course of her investigations, she has crossed paths with the [[AAA Fixers For You]]; despite the ominous threats leveled by [[Zimo the Cur]], she developed a friendly working relationship with the band of [[Fixer|Fixers]]. It was the group that escorted Fillipa through a dangerous cavern to discover the Vanda ruins outside [[Moradu]].
