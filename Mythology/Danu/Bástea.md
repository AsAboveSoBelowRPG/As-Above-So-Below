One of the most popular Goddess across all the [[Gods|pantheons]], Bástea is a [[Danu]] with major centers of worship in the [[Realm of the Sky]], the [[Surface Layer]], the [[Abyssal Depths]] and the [[Cthonic Remains]], making her the most widely worshipped God. Though a number of shrines and temples can be found in each of the [[Domains]] where she is present, her largest devotional center is found on the surface, in the [[Luavila (Region)|Capital Valley]] settlement of [[Conexão]] in [[Iberys]].

Known colloquially as "The Mother of Waters", as she gave birth to the three orders of elemental [[Rustic God|Rustic Gods]] associated with rivers, springs and seas - the [[Nymph]], [[Naiad]] and [[Nereid]].
